# Prestação de contas eleitorais

Campaign finance accounting: filing obligations, SPCE system, recibos
eleitorais, and the *aprovação / desaprovação / não prestação* outcomes.
For **substantive campaign finance rules** (donation limits, FEFC, ADI
4650), see `partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md` §6. For the **procedural
sequence** of prestação de contas review by the electoral courts, see
`processo-eleitoral.md` §2.

**Topics / keywords**: prestação de contas, campaign finance accounting,
SPCE, recibo eleitoral, quitação eleitoral, aprovação, desaprovação,
não prestação, sobras de campanha, fundo eleitoral, FEFC, despesa
eleitoral, doação, candidato, partido.

**Snapshot as of 2026**: filing via SPCE (Sistema de Prestação de Contas
Eleitorais). Thresholds and simplified-form rules updated by TSE
resoluções per election cycle — verify current values before using
specific R$ figures.

---

## 1. Filing obligations

- Each candidate must have a **campaign CNPJ** and open a dedicated
  bank account for campaign transactions.
- **All income** must be logged in SPCE within **72 hours** of receipt,
  accompanied by a *recibo eleitoral* (`LE.28.§4.I`).
- Recibo unnecessary for donations below the small-donation threshold
  (historically R$4,000; check current TSE resolução).
- **All expenditure** must be by check or bank transfer, except amounts
  below the small-expenditure threshold (historically R$300).

## 2. Expenditure limits

- Set by TSE resolução per election cycle, based on `L13488` and prior
  legislation.
- For the 2016 cycle: max 70% of the maximum spent in 2012 for the same
  position in the same municipality (`L13165.5`).
- Post-2018: inflation-adjusted monetary caps by office and
  municipality/state size.

## 3. Deadlines

- **Contas parciais** (interim): 72 hours for each receipt (`LE.28.§4.I`).
- **Contas finais**: 30 days after the election (`LE.28`).
- Elected candidates cannot be *diplomados* without their accounts
  having been *julgadas* (`LE.29.§2`).

## 4. Outcomes and effects

| Decision | Party effect | Candidate effect |
|---|---|---|
| Aprovação | None | None |
| Aprovação com ressalvas | Subject to proportionality | None |
| Desaprovação | Loss of fundo partidário (`LE.25`) | May trigger AIJE |
| Não prestação | Loss of fundo partidário | Impediment to *quitação eleitoral* (`LE.11.§7`) |

- Formal/material errors that have been corrected do not authorize
  desaprovação (`LE.30.§2`).
- Since 2010, even desaprovação generates *quitação eleitoral*; the
  practical sanction on the candidate is limited unless the MP pursues
  an AIJE.

## 5. Post-election obligations

- **Sobras de campanha**: surplus must be transferred to the party
  (`LE.31`).
- **Document retention**: at least 180 days after diplomação
  (`LE.32`). Suppression of documents triggers `CP.305`.
